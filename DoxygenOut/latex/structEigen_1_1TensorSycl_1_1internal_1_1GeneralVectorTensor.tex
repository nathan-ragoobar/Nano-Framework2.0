\hline

\hline
{\em Vector\+Mapper} & determines the tensor contraction mapper for the vector input (can be lhs or rhs)\\
\hline
{\em Tensor\+Mapper} & determines the tensor contraction mapper for the tensor input (can be lhs or rhs)\\
\hline
{\em Storage\+Index} & determines the Storage\+Index Type\\
\hline
{\em Properties} & determines the Contraction Panel properties\\
\hline
{\em KFactor} & determines the number of elements in K dimension in a Tile\\
\hline
{\em Vectorizable} & determines whether or not the vectorization is enabled for the \doxylink{namespaceEigen}{Eigen} expression.\\
\hline
{\em is\+\_\+lhs\+\_\+vec} & determines whether lhs is a vector or rhs is a vector\\
\hline
{\em Is\+Final} & determine if this is the final kernel. If so, the result will be written in a final output. Otherwise, the result of contraction will be written iin a temporary buffer.\\
\hline

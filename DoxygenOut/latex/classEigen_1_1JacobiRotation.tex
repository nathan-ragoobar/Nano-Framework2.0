\subsubsection*{template$<$typename Scalar$>$\newline
class Eigen\+::\+Jacobi\+Rotation$<$ Scalar $>$}
Rotation given by a cosine-\/sine pair. 

\textbackslash{}jacobi\+\_\+module

This class represents a Jacobi or Givens rotation. This is a 2D rotation in the plane {\ttfamily J} of angle $ \theta $ defined by its cosine {\ttfamily c} and sine {\ttfamily s} as follow\+: $ J = \left ( \begin{array}{cc} c & \overline s \\ -s  & \overline c \end{array} \right ) $

You can apply the respective counter-\/clockwise rotation to a column vector {\ttfamily v} by applying its adjoint on the left\+: $ v = J^* v $ that translates to the following \doxylink{namespaceEigen}{Eigen} code\+: 
\begin{DoxyCode}{0}
\DoxyCodeLine{\mbox{\hyperlink{namespaceabsl_a828e0f13fb3947cdf6406b7a4feec8aca9e3669d19b675bd57058fd4664205d2a}{v}}.applyOnTheLeft(J.adjoint());}

\end{DoxyCode}

Makes {\ttfamily \texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}this} as a Givens rotation {\ttfamily G} such that applying $ G^* $ to the left of the vector $ V = \left ( \begin{array}{c} p \\ q \end{array} \right )$ yields\+: $ G^* V = \left ( \begin{array}{c} r \\ 0 \end{array} \right )$.
